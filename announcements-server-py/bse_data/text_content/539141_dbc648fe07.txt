UFO Moviez Enters into a Strategic Alliance with Miraj Cinemas for Exclusive 
Advertising Rights Across 239 Screens in 49 Cities and 72 Locations 
 
Mumbai, 19th Nov 2025:  UFO Moviez, India’s largest in -cinema advertising network, 
today announced a strategic alliance with Miraj Cinemas, the country’s third -largest 
cinema exhibition chain. Through this partnership, UFO Moviez has secured exclusive 
advertising rights across Miraj Cinemas’ extensive network of 239 screens. 
 
With this collaboration, UFO Moviez’s total cinema advertising network will expand to over 
4000 screens, further strengthening its position as India’s largest integrator of in -cinema 
screen inventory. This partnership will also significantly enhance UFO’s multiplex screen 
portfolio, increasing it to over 2500 multiplex screens, and firmly consolidating its 
leadership as the largest multiplex on-screen advertising network in the country. 
 
This association is expected to combine Miraj Cinemas’ and UFO Moviez’s nationwide reach 
with UFO’s proven expertise in advertising and marketing, creating  new opportunities for 
brands to invest more strategically, aggressively, and consistently across this powerful 
network 
 
Commenting on the development, Mr. Rajesh Mishra , Executive Director  and 
Group CEO, UFO Moviez said- “We are pleased to announce a strategic partnership with 
Miraj Cinemas, through which we will expand our multiplex presence to over 2500 screens 
nationwide. This collaboration provides us with access to 239 additional multiplex screens, 
significantly strengthening our reach across key Tier 1 and Tier 2 markets. This partnership 
marks an important milestone in our growth journey and enables us to deliver even greater 
value, scale, and impact to our advertising partners”.

About Miraj Cinemas 
Miraj Cinemas is India’s third -largest and one of the fastest -growing cinema exhibition 
chains, known for delivering high -quality movie-viewing experiences across the country. 
With a robust presence spanning 239 screens across 49 cities and 72 locations, Miraj 
Cinemas continues to expand its footprint with a strong focus on state -of-the-art 
infrastructure, superior projection and sound technology, and exceptional customer 
service. The brand is committed to creating immersive entertainment environments that 
cater to diverse audiences. As part of the Miraj Group, the company continues to set new 
benchmarks in the Indian cinema exhibition industry through innovation, expansion, and 
a customer-first approach.
