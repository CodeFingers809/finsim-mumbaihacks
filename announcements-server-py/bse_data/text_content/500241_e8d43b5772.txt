This is with reference to our earlier intimation dated 17 February 2025 in the matter pursuant 
to the subject referred regulations read with Para A of Part A of Schedule III thereof, informing 
you that the majority of the Directors of the Company had received on 14 February, 2025, a 
notice dated 21 January 2025 under the Andhra Pradesh GST Act, 2017, allegedly claiming 
tax arrears of Rs. 15,48,06,528/ - for the year 2023 -24, associated with the transaction 
undertaken by an unincorporated Joint Venture viz. IVRCL -KBL-MEIL (JV) Hyderabad formed 
amongst the IVRCL Ltd., Company and Megha Engineering & Infrastructure Ltd. (MEIL) for 
execution of a Project with Water Resources Department of Government of Andhra Pradesh 
(erstwhile Irrigation and CAD Dept. of Government of Andhra Pradesh). 
 
Further, an u pdate was filed in this matter with the Stock Exchanges, informing that the 
Company has received a Demand Notice dated 5 August 2025 from the Assistant 
Commissioner (ST), Dwarakanagar Circle, Vishakhapatnam – 1, State Tax Department, 
Government of Andhra Pradesh for allegedly claiming tax arrears of Rs. 15,48,02,928/- for the 
year 2017-18 to be paid within 7 days from the date of receipt of the notice. Please note that 
the demand notice pertains to alleged arrears of Rs. 15,48,02,928/- for the year 2017-18 and 
not the alleged arrears of Rs. 15,48,06,528/- for the year 2023-24 as mentioned in their earlier 
notices dated 21 January 2025 issued to the majority of the Company’s Directors. 
 
Thereafter, the Compan y informed the stock exchanges on 12 August, 2025 about filing of 
Writ Petition on 12.08.2025 vide filing No. WP/31611/2025 challenging the said Demand 
Notice with a prayer to set aside the impugned assessment order and demand notice thereto 
along with int erim stay pending Writ Petition before  the Hon’ble High Court for the State of 
Andhra Pradesh at Amaravati (Special Original Jurisdiction).  
 
  
 
 
 
 
 
Subsequently, the Company has also informed the stock exchanges on August 27, 2025 that 
the Hon’ble High Court for State of Andhra Pradesh at Amaravati has granted an interim stay 
against the recovery proceedings, vide their order dated August 20, 2025 which has been 
made available to the Company on August 26, 2025. 
 
On November 19, 2025, it was also informed  that the Directors of the Company (who had 
been issued a demand notices under the Andhra Pradesh GST Act, 2017, allegedly claiming 
tax arrears) have filed Writ Petition (s) challenging the Demand Notice s with a prayer to set 
aside the impugned assessment or der and demand notice s thereto along with interim stay 
pending disposal of Writ Petition before the Hon’ble High Court for the State of Andhra 
Pradesh at Amaravati (Special Original Jurisdiction) vide filing No. WP/47631/2025. 
 
In this regard, we hereby inform that the Company has received  an Endorsement (Letter) 
dated November 20, 2025 (received by the Company  on November 20, 2025 at 03:43 p.m.) 
from The Deputy Assistant Commissioner of State Tax, Dwarakanagar Circle, Visakhapatnam-
I Division, informing that the alleged demand notices, issued to the directors of the Company, 
have been withdrawn. 
 
The details as required pursuant to Para B of Part A of Schedule III of the subject referred 
regulations read with SEBI’s Circular No. SEBI/HO/CFD/CFD -PoD-1/P/CIR/2023/123 dated
