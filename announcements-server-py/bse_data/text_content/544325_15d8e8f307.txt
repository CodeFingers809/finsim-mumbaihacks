Allotment of Shares under ITC Hotels – Special Purpose Employee Stock Option 
Scheme  
In terms of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015,  
we write to advise that the Company on 17th November, 2025 issued and allotted  
22,930 Equity Shares of ₹ 1/ - each, upon exercise of 2,293 Options by Optionees under  
ITC Hotels – Special Purpose Employee Stock Option Scheme. The Meeting of the  
Committee which allotted the Shares ended at 11:10 a.m.  
 
Consequently, with effect from 17th November, 2025, the Issued, Subscribed and  
Paid-up Share Capital of the Company stands increased to ₹ 2, 08,27,51,028/- divided into 
2,08,27,51,028 Equity Shares of ₹ 1/- each.
