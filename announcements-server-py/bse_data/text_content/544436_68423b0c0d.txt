Company proposes to utilize an estimated amount of Rs. 3.00 crore from the Net Proceeds towards 
repayment/ prepayment of some of the loan facilities availed by our Company (“Identified Loans”). 
Company may choose to repay/ prepay additional borrowings availed by the Company, which may include 
additional borrowings availed after the filing of this Prospectus. Given the nature of Identified Loans and 
the terms of repayment/prepayment, the aggregate outstanding borrowing amounts may vary from time to 
time. However, the aggregate amount to be utilized from the Net Proceeds towards repayment/ prepayment 
of the Identified Loans (excluding interest accrued thereon), in part or in full, would not exceed Rs. 3.00 
crore. 
In such cases or in case the Identified Loans (excluding interest thereon) is repaid/prepaid or refinanced 
prior to the completion of the issue, we may utilize Net Proceeds of the Issue towards repayment / 
prepayment of such additional and/ or re-financed indebtedness availed by us. 
3 Funding the capital expenditure requirement 
for expansion of our Dhinoj Facility 
(CAPEX) 
Company existing manufacturing unit is located at Survey No. 2554/2, covering 2,628 square meters, where 
castor oil manufacturing and allied activities are conducted. Additionally, the adjoining land and building at 
Survey No. 2537/2, covering 6770 square meters, will also be used to enhance our existing manufacturing 
capabilities. This includes setting up a Solvent Extraction Plant to extract residual oil from the byproduct that 
remains during the manufacturing of the castor oil from the existing manufactu ring facilities through 
investment in additional plant & machinery. Further, the production machinery, lines, and storage tanks of 
the existing plant and proposed solvent extract plant will be interconnected to each other to enhance the 
manufacturing of castor oil and its allied products. 
Company intends to enhance its existing manufacturing capabilities through investment in additional plant 
and machinery. These assets will be positioned at existing manufacturing unit located at Dhinoj, Gujarat. 
Company has received quotation for installation of plant and machinery from 3 suppliers to supply various 
parts of plant and machinery.
