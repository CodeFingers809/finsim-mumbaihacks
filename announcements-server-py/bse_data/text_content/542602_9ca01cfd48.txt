The Settlement Order was passed in connection with a Show 
Cause Notice dated May 30, 2025 , issued to the Manager and 
the Trustee by SEBI.  
The Show Cause Notice alleged (i) violation of Regulation 7(d) 
read with Clauses 1,2,7 and 8 of Schedule VI and Regulation 
10(14) of the SEBI REIT Regulations , with respect to alleged 
delays in the disclosure of the NFRA Order (by 53 days), the 
Manager’s view on the ‘fit and proper’ status of its erstwhile 
CEO (by 35 days) and a SEBI letter dated October 8, 2024 (by 
11 days) by the Manager and (ii) failure of t he Trustee to 
oversee the Manager and ensure compliance with Regulation 
7(d) read with Clauses 1,2 ,7 and 8 of Schedule VI and 
Regulations 9(3), 9(4) and 9(16)  of the SEBI REIT 
Regulations.  
The Manager filed a Settlement Application on June 13, 2025, 
without admitting or denying the findings of facts an d 
conclusions of law. Pursuant to a notice of demand issued by 
SEBI on October 13, 2025, the Manager remitted the settlement 
amount of ₹18,39,825, to SEBI.
