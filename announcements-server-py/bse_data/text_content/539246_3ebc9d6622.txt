Pursuant to Regulations 3 0 and 31A of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements)  Regulations, 
2015, we wish to inform you that the Board of Directors of Jyotirgamya Enterprises  Limited (“the Company”) at  
its meeting held on November 21, 2025 has considered and approved the  request received from Mr. Saeed UR 
Rehman seeking reclassification of his status from the  “Promoter / Promoter Group” category to the “Public” 
category. 
 
Based on the request and declaration submitted by Mr. Saeed UR Rehman, we hereby confirm the following: 
 
• He does not hold any equity shares in the Company as on the date; 
• He does not exercise any control over the Company, directly or indirectly;  
• He does not have any special rights, formal or informal, with respect to the Company;  
• He is not represented on the Board of the Company and does not act as Key Managerial Personnel; 
• He satisfies all conditions prescribed under Regulation 31A(3)(b) of the Listing  Regulations. 
 
In accordance with Regulation 31A(5) of the Listing Regulations, the Company shall submit the  application for 
reclassification to the Stock Exchange (BSE Limited) for obtaining its No  Objection, within the stipulated 
timeline.

Agenda:  
 
To consider and approve the request for the reclassification of Mr. Saeed UR Rehman  from 
“Promoter & Promoter Group” to “Public Category” under Regulation 31A of SEBI (LODR) 
Regulations, 2015. 
 
The Chairperson informed the Board that the Company had received a request from Mr. Saeed UR Rehman , 
one of the promoters of the Company, dated November 20, 2025, seeking  reclassification of its status from the 
“Promoter Category” to the “Public Category” pursuant to Regulation 31A of the SEBI (Listing 
Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 , as amended from time to time. 
 
The Chairperson further briefed the Board that Mr. Saeed UR Rehman: 
 
• Holds Nil equity shares (0%) in the Company; 
• Is not involved in the day-to-day management or affairs of the Company; 
• Meets all conditions specified under Regulation 31A(3)(b) of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015.  
  
The Promoter Shareholding (Before Reclassification): 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
* Mrs. Anju Chordia deceased on May 08, 2021 and in accordance with Regulation 31A(6)(c) of the SEBI 
(Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, in case of the death of a promoter or a 
person belonging to the promoter group, such person shall automatically cease to be included in the 
“Promoter / Promoter Group” category. The Company is currently in coordination with its Registrar and 
Share Transfer Agent (RTA) to initiate and complete the necessary formalities for the removal of her name 
from the promoter group records. Upon completion of the procedural formalities, her name shall be formally 
removed from the list of promoters in compliance with SEBI (LODR) Regulations.
