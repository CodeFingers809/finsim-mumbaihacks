Sub: Disclosure under Regulation 30 SEBI (LODR), 2015- Change in Management/ Directorate 
 
Pursuant to Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) 
Regulations, 2015, we hereby inform the resignation of Mrs. Neethu Subramoniyan 
(DIN:08788544) from the office of Independent Director with effect from 18th November 2025. 
 
The Board took note of the resignation of Mrs. Neethu Subramoniyan (DIN:08788544) with effect 
from 18th November 2025 via passing circular resolution on 18.11.2025. It was also noted by the 
Nomination and Remuneration Committee via passing circular resolution on 18.11.2025. 
Consequently, she shall also cease to be a Member of the Audit Committee, Nomination and 
Remuneration Committee, Stakeholders Relationship Committee, Corporate Social Responsibility 
Committee and Risk Management Committee of the Company w.e.f., 18.11.2025 and also cease to 
be the Chairman of Audit Committee and Corporate Social Responsibility Committee. 
 
The Company has received a confirmation from the resigning Director that there are no material 
reasons for her resignation other than those stated in the resignation letter. A copy of the 
resignation letter along with her confirmation is enclosed herewith, as required under SEBI 
Circular CIR/CFD/CMD/4/2015 dated 9 September 2015. 
 
The Board has taken note of the resulting vacancies in the Committees and will reconstitute the 
Committees upon appointment of a new Independent Director, in compliance with Regulations 
18, 19, 20 and 21 of SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 and Section 135 of the Companies Act, 2013. 
Further, in accordance with Regulation 25(6) of SEBI LODR Regulations, 2015, the Company shall 
fill the vacancy of the Independent Director within three months from the date of such vacancy 
or earlier once a suitable candidate is identified and appointed. 
 
 
 
The details required under Regulation 30 of the SEBI (LODR) Regulations, 2015 read with SEBI 
Circular No. CIR / CFD / CMD / 4 / 2015, dated September 09, 2015, are enclosed as Annexure 1.  
 
This disclosure is made in compliance with Regulation 30 of the Securities and Exchange Board 
of India (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 and the SEBI
