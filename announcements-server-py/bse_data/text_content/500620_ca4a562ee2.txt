PRESS RELEASE 
G E Shipping delivers its 2005 built Medium Range Product Tanker 
“Jag Pooja” 
 
The Great Eastern Shipping Company Limited (G E Shipping) delivered its 2005 built Medium 
Range Product Tanker “Jag Pooja” to the buyers . The vessel was contracted for sale in October 
2025. 
After this transaction, the company’s current owned fleet stands at 40 vessels, comprising 26 
tankers (6 crude tankers, 16 product tankers, 4 LPG carriers) and 14 dry bulk carriers (2 Capesize, 
10 Kamsarmax, 2 Supramax), aggregating 3.32 mn dwt. 
Additionally, the company has contracted to sell one Suezmax crude tanker and buy one 
secondhand Ultramax dry bulk carrier. These two transactions are expected to be completed in H2 
FY26.
