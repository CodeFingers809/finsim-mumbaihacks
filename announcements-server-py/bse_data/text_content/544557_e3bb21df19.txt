We wish to inform you of  that on 09 .07.2025, the Office of Commercial Tax Officer, Chennai had 
conducted an inspection under Section 67 of GST Act, 2017 on the premises of the company and seized 
few documents. Further summons were issued for FY 2019 -20, 2021 -22, and 2022 -23 for non -
remittance/short remittance of Reverse Charge Mechanism (RCM) on import of services.  
 
The Company had provided detailed workings  and submissions to the authorities in the month of July, 
2025. After adjustment of excess payments across the relevant periods, the balance RCM amounts for FY 
2019-20 and FY 2022-23 were remitted on 26.09.2025. All amounts are fully paid, and the matter has been 
concluded. 
 
The Company received the physical copy of the final order on 14th November 2025 at 5:00 p.m. The GST 
Department in order to fulfill their procedures had issued an order of seizure dated 07th November 2025 for  
seizure carried in the month of July 2025.
