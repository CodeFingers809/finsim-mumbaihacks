Aayush Wellness announces strategic partnership with Blinkit to 
strengthen its Distribution Network 
Expanding market reach in a Rapidly Growing USD 35 Billion Nutraceutical Market 
New Delhi, 1 7th November 2025: 
Aayush Wellness Ltd., an integrated 
health and wellness company, 
announced a significant milestone 
in its expansion strategy through a 
strategic partnership with Blinkit, 
one of India’s fastest-growing quick 
commerce platforms. This 
collaboration underscores Aayush 
Wellness’s commitment to 
reshaping its go-to-market Strategy 
by leveraging Blinkit’s extensive 
urban delivery network and 
advanced logistics infrastructure.  
The partnership will enable rapid 
and reliable product delivery, 
ensuring real -time availability and 
a seamless consumer experience. 
By integrating quick commerce capabilities into its distribution strategy, Aayush 
Wellness is set to position itself as an Omi-channel Healthcare Company.  
Strategic Rationale and Business Impact 
The Indian nutraceutical industry was valued at approximately USD 32 billion in 2024, 
is projected to surpass USD 75 billion by 2033, growing at a CAGR of around 10%. 
Capitalizing on this momentum, Aayush Wellness is accelerating its omnichannel growth 
through digital expansion and strategic partnerships that enhance accessibility, visibility, 
and efficiency. 
The collaboration with Blinkit represents a pivotal step in broadening Aayush Wellness’s 
market presence across major metropolitan and emerging urban centers. By harnessing 
Blinkit’s hyperlocal delivery ecosystem, the company can ensure that its products reach 
consumers within minutes , boosting brand recall, optimizing inventory management, 
and driving higher customer satisfaction.
