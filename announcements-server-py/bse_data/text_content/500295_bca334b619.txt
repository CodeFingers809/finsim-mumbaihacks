In line with Vedanta Limited’s commitment to financial excellence and capital efficiency, and pursuant 
to our broader objective of optimizing treasury operations, cost of funding, and liquidity management, 
we wish to inform you that a wholly owned subsidia ry of the Company, in the name of “ VEDANTA 
FINANCE IFSC LIMITED” has been incorporated in IFSC at GIFT City, Gujarat on November 18, 2025. 
 
The proposed subsidiary will serve as a strategic financial hub for Vedanta’s treasury and capital 
market operations, enhancing operational efficiency , trade  finance and fortifying its financial 
framework to thrive in a globally competitive landscape.
