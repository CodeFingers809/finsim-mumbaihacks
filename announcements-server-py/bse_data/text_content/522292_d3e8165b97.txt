Pursuant to Regulation - 30 of the SEBI (Listing Obligation & Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, we 
are pleased to inform you the following development of our Company: 
The company is pleased to announce that, it has received its first trial order for 6,700 meters of laminated fabrics 
from the approved Aerial Delivery Research and Development Establishment (“ADRDE”) vendor, MS Oriental 
Weaving & Processing Mills Pvt Ltd, Thane, Mumbai. This order is designated for final supply to ADRDE Agra, 
a laboratory under DRDO (Indian Defence Research and Development Organization). The end use of this fabric 
is for parachute manufacturing. This represents the first trial order received by Candour for the final supply of 
parachutes to ADRDE through Oriental Weaving & Processing Mills Pvt Ltd. 
We are further pleased to report that after undergoing rigorous inspection process, the processed laminated fabric 
produced by the Company has met ADRDE standards and received their official approval. With this approval, we 
are also pleased to communicate that ADRDE has now approached the company directly for the supply of coated 
and laminated fabrics featuring various special coatings and laminations and that ADRDE has formally confirmed 
the order for the first sampling supply of laminated fabrics. 
ADRDE specializes in the research and development of aerial delivery systems, including parachutes, heavy drop 
systems, and aerostats. It plays an essential role in national defense, supporting both the Indian Armed Forces and 
space programs such as ISRO's Gaganyaan Mission. 
As directed by ADRDE officials, the company has registered and gained approval on the Government E- 
Marketplace (GEM) portal, enabling official participation in tenders issued by DRDO, ADRDE, and multiple 
other government organizations. 
Additionally, today the company received further orders from Reliance Industries Ltd for 100,000 meters of 
lamination work on automotive fabrics, valued at approximately Rs 100 lakhs. The Company expects Completion 
of the same by March 2026, and discussions are underway regarding additional orders for FY 2026-27 from 
Reliance Industries Ltd.
