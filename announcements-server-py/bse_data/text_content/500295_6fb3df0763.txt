Subject: Vedanta Group Shines at LACP Spotlight Awards 2025 
 
Dear Sir/Ma’am, 
 
We are delighted to share that Vedanta Limited  (VEDL) and Hindustan Zinc Limited (HZL)  have 
achieved outstanding recognition at the prestigious LACP (League of American Communications 
Professionals) Spotlight Awards 2025 , setting new benchmarks in corporate reporting and 
communications with HZL and VEDL securing the No. 1 and No. 2 positions in India  and ranking 
among the world’s best corporate reports. 
 
HZL’s Integrated Annual Report FY  2024-25 was honored as Platinum Winner Worldwide  in the 
$1–10 billion revenue category, achieving 1
st position in India and 6th globally, with an outstanding 
score of 99/100 points. VEDL’s Integrated Annual Report FY 2024-25 earned the Gold Winner title 
in the Integrated Report category and Platinum Winner  for its Online/Digital Report under the 
$10+ billion revenue segment, ranking 2nd in India and 11th globally, with a score of 98/100 points 
in Integrated Report and 99/100 points in Online Report. Both companies feature d among the 
top 20 reports globally , making Vedanta Group the only Indian conglomerate to achieve this 
distinction. 
 
The awards, which attracted over 500 submissions from nearly a dozen countries, recognized our 
reports for sharp storytelling, contemporary design and clarity of messaging, setting benchmarks 
in transparent, shareholder -centric communication. This achievement reflects Vedanta Group’s 
unwavering commitment to excellence, ESG leadership, and global best practices in corporate 
reporting.
