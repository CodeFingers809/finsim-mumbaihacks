PDS Limited’s Annual Report Wins Gold at LACP 2025 Spotlight Awards, 
Moves Up to Rank 46 Globally 
 
Mumbai, 18th Nov 2025: PDS Limited, the leading global fashion supply chain solutions company, is pleased to 
announce that PDS’ Annual Report for FY 2024 -25 has been awarded  the Gold Award in  the 2025 Spotlight 
Awards - Global Communications Competition by the League of American Communications Professionals (LACP) 
LLP. The award recognises excellence in corporate communications for the company’s Annual Report titled 
“Weaving Threads of Trust.” 
PDS has secured an exceptional score of 98 out of 100 this year, placing the company prominently within the 
Top 100 Annual Reports worldwide. Elevating this success further, PDS’ Annual Report moved up to a global rank 
of 46, a significant jump from its 2023 position of 74.  
“We are delighted to be recognised once again among LACP’s Top 100 Annual Reports worldwide ,” said Sanjay 
Jain, Group CEO. “Moving up to the 46th rank is a proud achievement for PDS and reflects our commitment to 
strong communication through transparency, and world-class reporting standards. This award is a testament to 
the collaborative effort behind our Annual Report.” 
The LACP Spotlight Awards is a globally renowned platform that recognises excellence in communication across 
industries. This year’s competition received submissions from 20 countries across four continents.  
About PDS Limited: (www.pdsltd.com)  
PDS Limited is a global fashion infrastructure platform offering product development, sourcing, manufacturing, 
and distribution for major brands and retailers worldwide handling over $2.2 billion of Gross Merchandise Value. 
The Company operates a vast global network covering over 90 offices in 22 countries, with over 4,500 employees 
and 6,000 factory associates worldwide. PDS also offers a bespoke end -to-end outsourcing solution, engaging 
dedicated talent and infrastructure as an extended arm of retailers a nd brands. The Company reported 
consolidated revenues of ₹12,578cr in FY25.
