MEDIA RELEASE 
Kolhapur, 19 November 2025 
Deva Thapa Crowned ‘Rose Merc Kesari’ as Two Maharashtra Kesaris Fight to Thrilling Draw at Sondoli   
The traditional wrestling championship organized by the villagers of Sondoli on the auspicious occasion of the 
Shri Nayakeshwar Deva Yatra concluded on a high note yesterday, drawing a record -breaking crowd and 
electrifying the historic Sondoli wrestling arena. 
Powered by   Rose Merc Limited, Emirates Holding Group   and   Jadhav Rose Merc Sports Pvt. Ltd., and held 
under the patronage of Director   Mr. Dattatray Jadhav, the day-long mud-wrestling spectacle lived up to its 
reputation as one of Maharashtra’s most prestigious rural dangals. 
Headline Bout Ends in Gripping Draw     
In a pulsating clash of titans, two -time Maharashtra Kesari Prithviraj Patil and reigning Maharashtra Kesari 
Harshavardhan Sadgir fought to a thrilling draw. Both champions displayed extraordinary strength, technique 
and stamina, leaving the packed arena on its feet throughout the bout. 
Satpal Sontakke Lifts ‘Sondoli Kesari’ Title     
In the battle for the coveted ‘Sondoli Kesari’ title, Satpal Sontakke dominated Sameer Shaikh with relentless 
attacking wrestling to claim the championship. 
Nepal’s Deva Thapa Steals the Show     
The showstopper of the evening was the special attraction bout between Nepal’s celebrated wrestler   Deva 
Thapa and India’s aggressive powerhouse Amit Lakha Thapa produced a commanding performance to win 
convincingly and was crowned the prestigious ‘Rose Merc Kesari’.   
He was felicitated with a silver mace (gada) and cash prize by Rose Merc Limited Director - Mr. Purvesh 
Shelatkar and Mr. Qazi Abdul Matin. 
Other Notable Results     
- Kumar Patil def. Sunil Karwate (spectacular back-throw)   
- Hari Markand def. Suraj Patil (on points)   
- Sahil Patil def. Dhananjay Patil (superb one-wheel throw)   
- Ajay Shedge def. Pradeep Thakur   

 
Victories were also recorded by Ajay Patil, Vijay Sarde, Ajit Patil, Datta Bankar, Omkar Jadhav, Ashutosh Lad, 
Atharva Bhosale and Shri Chormare in their respective bouts. 
The tournament began with the traditional worship of the akhara, performed by Sarpanch Mr. Bhimrao Patil, 
Deputy Sarpanch   Mr. Shivaji Sawant   and other village dignitaries. 
Rose Merc Limited Director Mr. Purvesh Shelatkar said: “We are proud to support and preserve the rich 
wrestling heritage of Kolhapur that was patronized by Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj. Events like Sondoli Dangal 
keep this glorious tradition alive for future generations.” 
Jadhav Rose Merc Sports Pvt. Ltd. Director Mr. Dattatray Jadhav added: “Organizing a grand dangal in my 
own village has been a lifelong dream. After the overwhelming success here, we are now preparing to host 
an international-standard mud-wrestling championship in Dubai, which will open global doors for wrestlers 
from our region.” 
A giant LED screen was installed for the first time at Sondoli, ensuring every spectator enjoyed a clear view of 
the action. The event received wholehearted support from the Sarpanch, Deputy Sarpanch, Gram Panchayat 
members and the entire Sondoli community, including residents based in Mumbai. 
Prominent dignitaries present included former MLA Mr. Satyajit Patil, industrialist Mr. Qazi Abdul Matin, Mr. 
Purvesh Shelatkar and Mr. D.R. Jadhav. 
The bouts were officiated with utmost fairness by the referee panel comprising Shankar Patil, Amar Patil, 
Suraj Sawant, Vikas Sawant, Ananda Patil and Satish Kadam. 
About Rose Merc Limited  
Rose Merc Limited is a leading Indian multinational conglomerate with diversified interests in International 
Events and trade, sports promotion and sport activities. Deeply committed to preserving and promoting 
India’s traditional sports, particularly the historic wrestling (kushti) heritage of Kolhapur, the group actively 
supports rural dangals and emerging wrestlers across Maharashtra and beyond through its sports arm, Jadhav 
Rose Merc Sports Pvt. Ltd. 
About Jadhav Rose Merc Sports Pvt. Ltd 
Jadhav Rose Merc Sports Pvt. Ltd. is a Maharashtra-based venture jointly promoted by Mr. Dattatray Jadhav 
and Rose Merc Limited, focused on organizing and managing sports events, leagues, and fitness initiatives to 
promote Maharashtra’s traditional and modern sporting culture.
