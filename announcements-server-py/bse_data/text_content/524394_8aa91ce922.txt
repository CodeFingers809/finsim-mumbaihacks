Sub Intimation of Shares Transferred by “Andhra Pradesh Industrial Development Corporation
Limited” (“APIDC”) to “Telangana State Industrial Development Corporation Limited” (“TSIDC”)
Ref Our Intimation to Stock Exchanges dated 13” August 2023 - Intimation of Disclosure of
Dispute Termination of Investment Agreement dated 27th June 1991.
With reference to the subject cited above and pursuant to Regulation 30 and Regulation 31A of the
Securities and Exchange Board of India (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements)
Regulations, 2015 (“SEBI LODR”), this is to inform you that the shares held by “Andhra Pradesh
Industrial Development Corporation Limited” (“APIDC”) have been transferred to “Telangana State
Industrial Development Corporation Limited” (“TSIDC”) w.e.f., 18” November 2025. The said
transfer is pursuant to the bifurcation of the erstwhile State of Andhra Pradesh into the Residual
Andhra Pradesh and the State of Telangana, and the Demerger Scheme of APIDC as per the
provisions of Section 68 read with Section 53 and the Ninth Schedule of the Andhra Pradesh
Reorganisation Act, 2014.
In accordance with Regulation 31A of the SEBI LODR, APIDC has been classified as a Promoter
pursuant to the Investment Agreement dated 27th June 1991. Since the matter remains sub judice
before the appropriate Judicial Authority and the shares held by APIDC have now been transferred
to TSIDC, the classification of the entities is proposed to be updated accordingly. TSIDC shall be
categorized as ‘Promoter — Body Corporate,’ and APIDC shall be re classified under the ‘Public’
shareholder category. Further, we wish to inform you that there is no other change to the stock
exchange intimation made on 13th August 2023 regarding the Intimation of Disclosure of
Dispute/Termination of the Investment Agreement dated 27th June 1991.
This is for your information and records.
