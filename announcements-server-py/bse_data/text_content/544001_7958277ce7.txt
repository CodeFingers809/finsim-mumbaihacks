PRESS RELEASE  
 
 Acquisition done by Sunita Tools Limited in Avisan Group 
 
Mumbai, November 20, 2025, Sunita Tools Limited now developing into a major defence Player. Sunita 
tools has over 40 years of presence in engineering field and now with Its Artillery Shells factory almost 
ready for commercial production, Sunita tools now ventures in Naval and Ship building Part of the Defence 
Industry. 
 
Accordingly, Sunita Tools limited Acquires a majority stake in the companies Avisan Global Tech Private 
limited & Avisan Engineering Enterprises, Avisan Group is into Weapons Installation, Ship building, Ship 
retrofitting and other aspects of naval defence and ship building Industry. 
 
Avisan Group has combined sales of over 6.27cr in FY 25, It has an order book of over 20.53 crores from 
very reputed defence and ship building companies. Viz Navy Weapons Dept, Hindustan Ship yard,  Goa 
Shipyard, Cummins, Larsen & Toubro etc. 
 
The orders range from Installing Weapons Systems on various Naval Ships, replace main surveillance radar 
on INS Vikrant, Replace Sonar radars and other weapons systems on various naval ships, Installing Missile 
systems in various Naval ships etc. 
 
Sunita Tools and Avisan together look forward to the opening up of the Ship building, Ship Retrofitting 
and Naval defence industry and cater to their needs and grow substantially over the next few years.
