In continuation of our intimations dated September 30, 2025 , October 30, 2025 October 31, 
2025 November 1, 2025 and pursuant to Regulations 30 and 33 of the SEBI (Listing 
Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 (“Listing Regulations”), we wish 
to inform you as follows: 
1. The Hon’ble National Company Law Tribunal, Mumbai Bench, vide its Order dated October 
10, 2025, sanctioned the Composite Scheme of Arrangement and Amalgamation involving: 
 Allcargo Supply Chain Private Limited (Transferor Company 1) 
 Gati Express and Supply Chain Private Limited (Transferor Company 2) 
 Allcargo Gati Limited (Transferor Company 3 / Transferee Company 1) 
 Allcargo Logistics Limited (Transferee Company 2 / Demerged Company) 
The Scheme became effective upon filing the certified copy of the Order with the Registrar 
of Companies, Mumbai, on November 1, 2025 (“Effective Date”) pursuant to Clause 40 of 
the Scheme and Section 232(5) of the Companies Act, 2013. 
2. Consequently, Allcargo Gati Limited has been amalgamated with and into Allcargo 
Logistics Limited and has ceased to exist as a separate legal entity from the Effective Date. 
 
3. In terms of the said Scheme, the Board of Directors of Allcargo Logistics Limited (“the 
Transferee Company”) at its meeting held on Friday, November 14, 2025, has, inter-alia: 
 Approved the Unaudited Standalone and Consolidated Financial Results of Allcargo 
Gati Limited (Transferor Company) for the second quarter and half year ended 
September 30, 2025, along with the Limited Review Reports issued by the Statutory 
Auditors, pursuant to the provisions of the Composite Scheme and the applicable 
accounting standards. 
 Noted that the financial results of Allcargo Gati Limited for the said period shall form 
part of the consolidated financial statements of Allcargo Logistics Limited, in 
accordance with the Scheme.
