Summary of the proceedings of the resolution passed by way of postal ballot by 
the Shareholders of Valiant Communications Limited 
Valiant Communications Limited (the "Company") at the Board Meeting held on October 16, 2025 
decided to obtain consent of the Members through Postal Ballot under Section 110 of the 
Companies Act, 2013 read with Rule 20 and 22 of the Companies (Management and 
Administration) Rules, 2014, in respect of issuance of up to 6,00,000 fully convertible warrants to 
the persons/ entities belonging to the"public"category on preferential basis 
The Postal Ballot notice dated October 16, 2025 was sent to Members of Company whose names 
appeared on the Register of Members/List of beneficiaries as on October 10, 2025 (Cut-Off date). 
The Company as on October 17, 2025, has completed sending Postal Ballot Notice in electronic 
form only through MUFG lntime India Pvt. Ltd. to the members/ shareholders who have 
registered their E-mail ID's with depositories or with the Company. The Company also 
provided remote e-voting facility for its Members, to enable them to cast their votes 
electronically instead of submitting the Postal Ballot form and availed the services of MUFG 
lntime India Pvt. Ltd. to provide the facility to the members fore-voting. 
The e-voting period commenced on Sunday, October 19, 2025 at 9.00 A.M. (IST) and ended on 
Monday, November 17, 2025 at 5.00 P.M. (IST). 
The Board had appointed Mr. Mahesh Kumar Gupta, Company Secretary in Practice (FCS- 2870 
and CP No: 1999) as the scrutinizer ("Scrutinizer") for conducting the e-voting process in a 
fair and transparent manner. The scrutinizer carried out the scrutiny of the votes polled 
electronically up to November 17, 2025 and submitted his report dated November 18, 
2025. 
Based on the Scrutinizer report, we confirm that following resolution as contained in the Postal 
Ballot Notice dated October 16, 2025 was passed by the Members with requisite majority and is 
effective from November 17, 2025.
