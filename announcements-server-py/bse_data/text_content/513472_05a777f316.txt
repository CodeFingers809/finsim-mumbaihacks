Simplex Castings Limited Secures Prestigious Orders from 
Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited and Paharpur Cooling Towers  
 
 
Bhilai, 19th November 2025: Simplex Castings Limited (BSE: 513472 ) India’s leading 
Precision Metallurgy company is pleased to announce the receipt of two significant fabrication 
orders. These orders underscore the company’s strong position and ongoing commitment to 
supporting India's infrastructure and industrial growth.  
Order Details: 
1. Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL), Trichy: 
Simplex has been awarded a fabrication contract valued at ₹11.76 Crores for the supply of 
1401 MT of Column, Auto Weld, and General Fabrication on a Vendor Material (OVM) basis 
for the DVC Raghunathpur Projects. The execution period for this order is 10 months. 
2. Paharpur Cooling Towers Limited: 
Simplex has also secured an order worth ₹80.73 Lakhs for fabrication and supply of 90 MT of 
supporting structures, handrails, and crate fabrication with raw material and primer to be 
delivered in 3 months. 
Both contracts are from reputable domestic entities and do not constitute related party 
transactions. 
Mr. Ketan Shah, Promoter, Chairman and Executive Director commente d: “Securing 
orders from esteemed clients like Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL) and Paharpur 
Cooling Towers Limited has demonstrated confidence and expertise of  Simplex Castings 
Limited. These contracts highlight our credibility in the market and have a positive impact on 
our business outlook, strengthening our order book and revenue visibility over the next several 
quarters. Our proven track record will continue to drive new order inflows from other marquee 
clients and new sectors, and we’re well placed to fulfil these new opportunities.” 
About Simplex Castings Limited: 
Simplex Castings Limited (SCL) is a leading Indian Precision Metallurgy company with global 
business presence. SCL possesses well equipped manufacturing  facilities such as Cast Iron 
Foundry, and Heavy Engineering  & Fabrication Plant. Each plant is associated with modern  
machining facilities and a central machine shop with  several machine tools including a large 
number of CNCs,  EPC Division to take up Turn -Key Projects, Design wing with  modern 
computer setup and aided tools. Website: simplexcastings.com
