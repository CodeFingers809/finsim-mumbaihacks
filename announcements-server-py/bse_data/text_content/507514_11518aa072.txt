SUBJECT: Acquisition of Entire Equity of Woodpecker Greenagri Nutrients Private 
Limited (WGNPL) and Making it a Wholly Owned Subsidiary Company (WOS) – U.P. 
Project Update  - Disclosure/Intimation under Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) 
Regulations 2015 
 
This is in furtherance to outcome of Board meeting of the Company on 13.11.2025  with 
reference to t he boards proposal to acquire entire  equity holding of Woodpecker Greenagri 
Nutrients Private Limited (WGNPL)  (an integrated Alcobev m anufacturing project at 
Farrukhabad Distt. UP) and make it a Wholly Owned Subsidiary of the Company.  
 
Pursuant to the requirement of Regulation 30 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure 
Requirements) Regulations, 2015, we would like to inform you that the WGNPL’s Board at its 
meeting held on today ie Monday, November 17, 2025, considered and approved the transfer 
of Equity to Som Distilleries and Breweries Limited (the Company SDBL) consequently 
Company WGNPL has become an Wholly Owned Subsidiary (“WOS”) of the Company SDBL. 
 
Phase I of the WGNPL’s project comprises the establishment of a brewery, which will ensure 
that SDBL’s products are easily available in UP and northern region of the country, thereby 
enhancing the sales and market reach of SDBL’s beer brands . In Phase II, WGNPL will also 
set up a distillery at the project site to further strengthen the Company’s production 
capabilities and product portfolio.  
 
This move will also ensure that the Company achieves better operational alignment, 
streamlined decision-making, and enhanced strategic integration with SDBL. It will further 
enable optimal utilization of resources, improved governance, and a unified approach toward 
future expansion and value creation for all stakeholders.
