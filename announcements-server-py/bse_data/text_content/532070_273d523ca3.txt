Summary of proceedings of the Court Convened Meeting of the Equity Shareholders of the Company 
 
The Court convened Meeting of the Equity Shareholders of Sumuka Agro Industries Limited (Formerly 
known as Superb Papers Limited) was held on Tuesday, November 18, 2025 at 03:00 P.M. through two-way 
Video Conferencing (VC)/Other Audio -Visual Means  (OAVM) in accordance with Circulars issued by the 
Ministry of Corporate Affairs and Securities and Exchange Board of India. 
 
The NCLT, Mumbai bench has appointed me Mr. Paresh Harishkumar Thakker, as the Chairperson of this 
Meeting . Mr. Paresh Harishkumar Thakker, welcomed the Equity Shareholders to the Meeting convened in 
accordance with the Order  dated September22,  2025  passed  in  COMP.APPL/ 279(MB)2025in 
C.A.(CAA)/187(MB)2025 and  order dated August25, 2025  passed in CA (CAA)/187/(MB)/2025  of the 
Hon’ble National Company Law Tribunal, Mumbai Bench and briefed them on key points relating to the 
participation in the Meeting through VC. He informed the Members that the Company had provided facility 
to cast their vote electronically through the National Securities Depository Limited (‘NSDL’) system before 
the Meeting. The e -voting commenced on Saturday,  November  15,  2025  at 9.00 A.M. IST and ended on 
Monday, November 17, 2025  at 5.00 P.M. and that the remote e -voting facility was also made available 
during the Meeting for the benefit of Members who were present during the Meeting and had not cast 
their votes earlier through remote e-voting. 
 
Mr. Amit Rathi, Independent Director , Ms. Shilpa Mathi, Director and Ms. Shaili Patel, Director  and CFO, 
attended the Meeting through VC at Mumbai.  
 
After introduction by the Directors of the Company, Mr. Paresh Harishkumar Thakker  welcomed the 
Members to the Meeting and as the requisite quorum as per section 103 of the Companies Act, 2013 was 
present called the meeting to order. 
 
Mr. Paresh Harishkumar Thakker  then explained the salient features of the Scheme of Merger by 
Absorption of Gujjubhai Foods Private Limited (“Transferor Company” or “First Applicant Company”) with 
Sumuka Agro Industries Limited (“ Transferee Company or “Second Applicant Company”) and their 
respective shareholders. This Scheme will lead to consolidation of business and assets, synergy of 
operations and networks of both the Companies. This will help achieve better  and more efficient utilization 
of available resources, benefits of internal economies, diversification to mitigate risks and improving 
organizational efficiencies 
 
Upon receipt of regulatory approvals viz. BSE/SEBI and National Company Law Tribunal and the entire 
Scheme becoming effective,
