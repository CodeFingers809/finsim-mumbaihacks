Subject: Intimation under Regulation 30 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure

Pursuant to Regulation 30 read with Schedule III of the Listing Regulations, this is to inform 
you that the Mankind Pharma Limited (“Company”) has received an order on November 14, 
2025 from office of the Commissioner Goods and Service Tax, Haryana issued by Additional 
Commissioner, CGST, Gurugram, Haryana, under Section 73 of the Central Goods and 
Services Tax Act, 2017 (“CGST Act)”.  
 
Based on the assessment of facts and prevailing law, the Company is of the view that the 
aforesaid order is arbitrary and unjustified. The Company will file necessary appeal with the 
appellate authority in this regard. There is no material impact on financials, operations or other 
activities of the Company. 
 
The detailed disclosure as required under Regulation 30 of the Listing Regulations read with 
SEBI Master Circular No. SEBI/HO/CFD/PoD2/CIR/P/0155 dated November 11, 2024 is
