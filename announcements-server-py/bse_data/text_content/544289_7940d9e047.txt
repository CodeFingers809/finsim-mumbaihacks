Ayana Renewable Power Private Limited (Ayana) a wholly owned subsidiary of ONGC 
NTPC Green Private Limited  (50:50 JV between N TPC Green Energy Limited and ONGC 
Green Limited) wins 140 MW Round The Clock RE Project in REMC Limited Tender. 
 
New Delhi, 21st November 2025 
 
Ayana Renewable Power Private Limited (Ayana), a wholly owned subsidiary of ONGC NTPC 
Green Private Limited has emerged as a successful bidder in the e-reverse auction conducted by 
REMC Limited. The tender aimed at the “Selection of Project Developers for Supply of 1000 MW 
of Round-the-Clock (RTC) Power from Grid-Connected Renewable Energy (RE) Power Projects 
with or without Storage”. 
 
Ayana secured a capacity of 140 MW at a tariff of ₹4.35/kWh. In line with the Request for Selection 
(RFS) conditions,  
 
The auction was concluded on 21st November 2025.
