The existing main objects of the Company, as set out in Clause 3(a) of the Memorandum of Association, enable 
the Company to carry on the business as Mutual Fund Agent and provide consultancy and advisory services in 
the field of portfolio management, stock broking, mutual funds, financial products, and related financial services. 
In view of the evolving financial services landscape and increasing opportunities in the areas of Alternative 
Investment Funds (AIFs), Portfolio Management Services (PMS), and franchise -based distribution models, the 
Board of Directors proposes to expand the scope of the Company ’s main objects to include the facilitation, 
promotion, and support of distribution of AIF and PMS schemes, and to enable the Company to operate as an 
intermediary or facilitator in India as well as in International Financial Services Centres (IFSC) ([as specified in 
sub-clauses 3(a)(2) to 3(a)(5) of the above-mentioned Special Resolution] 
 
The proposed insertion of new sub -clauses 3(a)(2) to 3(a)(5) in the main objects clause will empower the 
Company to: 
 Facilitate and support the distribution and marketing of AIF and PMS scheme s through franchise and 
digital platforms; 
 Enter into collaborations and partnerships with mutual fund companies, portfolio managers, and other 
financial institutions; 
 Receive and share commission, fees, brokerage, and remuneration in compliance with regulatory 
guidelines; and 
 Establish and operate digital and physical platforms connecting investors with authorized distributors and 
agents. 
 
The proposed additions will enable business diversification and strengthen the Company ’s presence in the 
financial intermediation and wealth management sectors, in alignmen t with SEBI and IFSCA regulatory 
frameworks. 
The Board at its meeting held on November 12, 2025 has approved to alter the above-mentioned Object Clause 
of the MOA of the Company, subject to approval of the shareholders of the Company through ensuing Posta l 
Ballot and sanction of Registrar of Companies, appropriate authorities, departments or bodies as and to the extent 
necessary.

Members of the Company is sought by way of Special Resolution for alteration of above-mentioned Object 
Clauses of MOA of the Company.
