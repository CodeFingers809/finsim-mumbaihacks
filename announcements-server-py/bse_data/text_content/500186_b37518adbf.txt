About Hindustan Oil Exploration Company Ltd. 
Hindustan Oil Exploration Company Limited (HOEC) is one of the pioneer private players in India’s Oil & Gas Exploration 
and Production (E&P) sector. HOEC holds a diverse portfolio comprising 10 oil and gas blocks with discovered 
resources and one exploratory block, strategically located across four of the seven producing basins in India. With over 
four decades of rich sectoral experience, HOEC's vision is to establish and transform resources into assets  and unlock 
value for all its stakeholders.
