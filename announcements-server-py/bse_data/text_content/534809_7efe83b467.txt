Public Category’, after adjusting the number of shares, paid-up value per share and premium per share post sub-division / split

per Warrant (being 75% of the Issue Price per Warrant) pursuant to the exercise of their right of conversion of Warrants into 
equity shares in accordance with the provisions of the SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 
2018. The newly allotted equity shares shall rank pari-passu with the existing equity shares of the Company.
