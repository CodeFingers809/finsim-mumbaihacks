Brief profile of Mayank Pandey 
 
Mayank Pandey is a seasoned business leader with over 25 
years of experience across oil & gas, lubricants, consulting, 
paints, and engineering sectors. He currently serves as Vice -
President at bp’s Customers & Products (C&P) Business & 
Technology Centre, where he drives strategic transformation, 
operational excellence, and innovation initiatives across bp’s 
global C&P business. 
 
Previously, Mayank was Whole -Time Director and Vice -President & Head  – Supply 
Chain at Castrol India Limited ( August 2021 to July 2024). As a member of the Castrol 
India Board and leadership team, he strengthen ed supply chain competitiveness, 
accelerated digital transformation, and deliver ed business growth. Under his 
leadership, the supply chain organisation earned recognition for its focus on safety, 
reliability, and performance. 
 
Mayank joined bp/Castrol in 2007 and has since held multiple senior leadership roles 
over 18 years, leading business teams and transformation programmes across India, 
China, Japan, Korea, and Europe. His experience reflects a strong track record of 
operating in diverse markets and cultures. Before bp /Castrol, he worked with Tata 
Strategic Management Group, Asian Paints, and Larsen & Toubro, building expertise in 
business strategy, operations, and organisational development. 
 
Mayank holds a Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from Harcourt Butler 
Technological Institute and an MBA from the S.P. Jain Institute of Management & 
Research.
