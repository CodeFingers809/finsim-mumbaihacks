Dear Sirs,  
Enclosed is a disclosure by Catalyst Trusteeship Limited under Regulation 29(2) & 29(4) of the SEBI 
(Substantial Acquisition of Shares and Takeovers) Regulations, 2011 (the “Takeover Code”).  
This disclosure is in respect of the release over pledged 63,050,966 equity shares, constituting 
9.93% of the paid -up share capital of Apollo Tyres Limited, created by Emerald Sage Investment 
Ltd in favour of Catalyst Trusteeship Limited (acting in the capacity of the security agent) for the 
benefit of certain lenders including their assigns, transferees, successors and novates from time to 
time, provided they are overseas banks or otherwise eligible to obtain the benefit of the pledge under 
applicable Reserve Bank of India guidelines and their agent/trustees.
