Investment Trusts) Regulations, 2014 (“SEBI InvIT Regulations”), read with SEBI Master Circular for

the Board of Directors (“Board”) of Maple Infra InvIT Investment Manager Private Limited (“Company” 
or “Investment Manager”), acting in the capacity of the Investment Manager to Maple Infrastructure Trust 
(“MIT”), through circular resolution passed on November 20, 2025 , has approved the allotment of 
120,603,200 units of MIT at an issue price of INR 145.6  per unit aggregating to an amount up to INR 
17,559,825,920/- to the allottees listed in Annexure-A on preferential basis. 
 
The aforesaid units shall rank pari passu in all respects including entitlement to distributions, voting rights 
or otherwise, with the existing units of MIT. The units allotted pursuant to the preferential issue shall be 
locked-in for such time period as prescribed under SEBI InvIT Regulations and the SEBI Master Circular 
and also be listed on BSE Limited, Stock Exchange.
