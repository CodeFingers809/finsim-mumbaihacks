Sub: Outcome of Board Meeting held on Friday, November 21, 2025 
 
 
Pursuant to Regulation 30  read with Schedule III  of the Securities and Exchange Board of India (Listing Obligations 
and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 (the “ SEBI Listing Regulations”), we wish to inform you that a 
Board of Directors of the Company in its meeting held today i.e. Friday, November 21, 2025, at the registered office 
of the company, inter alia, has considered and approved the following matters : 
 
1. The re-appointment of Mr.  Tejas Kothari (DIN: 01308288) as the Joint Managing Director of the Company for a 
further term of 5 years commencing from December 31 , 2025 together with the remuneration and terms of 
appointment as recommended by the Nomination & Remuneration Committee  subject to the approval of 
shareholders through postal ballot. 
 
2. The re-appointment of Mr. Siddhartha Xavier  (DIN: 03166884) as the Joint Managing Director of the Company for 
a further term of 5 years commencing from December 31, 2025 together with the remuneration and terms of 
appointment as recommended by the Nomination & Remuneration Committee  subject to the approval of 
shareholders through postal ballot. 
 
3. The re -appointment of Mr. Aditya Xavier (DIN: 06479014) as the Whole-time Director of the Company for a 
further term of 5 years commencing from December 31, 2025 together with the remuneration and terms of 
appointment as recommended by the Nomination & Remuneration Committee  subject to the approval of 
shareholders through postal ballot. 
 
4. The Board reviewed and approved the revised Draft Notice of Postal Ballot to seek the necessary approval of the 
members for the aforesaid transactions, together with the additional transactions approved by the Board at its 
meeting held on November 14, 2025. 
 
The details with respect to the Re -appointment of Managing Director & Whole Time Director  required under SEBI
