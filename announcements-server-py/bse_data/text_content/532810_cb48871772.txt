Madam/Sir.
This is to inform that the following Special Purpose Vehicle (SPY) Company has been incorporated as wholly
owned subsidiary of PFC Consulting Limited (PFCCL) (a wholly owned subsidiary of Power Finance Corporation
Limited (Pf-C)):
Sr. No. Name of the SPV Purpose 
I. R/\IGAD POWER for '·Establishment of 765/400/220 kV AIS Kandalgaon
TRANSMISSION LIMITED (Dist. Raigad)"
Maharashtra State Electricity Transmission Company Limited (MSETCL) has placed Letter of Intent (LO I) dated
14.08.2025 appointing PFC Consulting Limited (PFCCL) as Bid Process Coordinator (BPC) for the purpose of
selection of Bickler as Transmission Service Provider (TSP) to establish the aforementioned transmission system
through Tari IT Based Competitive Bidding Process.
As per the provisions of tariff based competitive bidding Guidelines issued by the Ministry of Power, the BPC
has to prepare the project profile and initiate the process of land acquisition and ti.west clearance, if required. For
these activities. a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) is required to be incorporated which will take up various
preparatory activities of the transmission projects e.g. survey and preparation of report, initialization of the process
of acquisition of land and also initiate the process of seeking forest clearance, if required.
The SPY has to be transferred to the Successful Bidder selected through International Competitive Bidding
Process under Tariff Based Competitive Bidding Guidelines for Transmission Service issued by Ministry of
Power.
Accordingly. the above SPY has been incorporated as a wholly owned subsidiary of PFCCL to develop the
aforementioned transmission scheme. The SPY will be transferred to the successful bidder after the completion
of the bidding process. The successful bidder selected through the bidding process would develop the project.
