Sub: Allotment of Equity Shares under TDPSL Equity Based Compensation Plan 2019 
 
The Nomination and Remuneration Committee of the Board has, by a resolution passed today i.e. 
November 18, 2025, approved allotment of 22,526 Equity Shares Rs.2/- each, at par, pursuant to 
the exercise of employee stock appreciation rights (ESAR) under TDPSL Equity Based 
Compensation Plan 2019. 
 
Consequent to the aforesaid allotment, the paid-up share capital of the Company stands increased 
from Rs.31,23,84,608/- (15,61,92,304 Equity Shares face value of Rs.2/- each) to 
Rs.31,24,29,660/- (15,62,14,830 Equity Shares face value of Rs.2/- each). 
 
In terms of Regulation 10(c) of the SEBI (Share Based Employee Benefits and Sweat Equity) 
Regulations, 2021, the statement on details of shares allotted is enclosed. Further, the intimation 
is also to be considered in terms of Regulation 30 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure 
Requirements) Regulations, 2015.  
 
The aforesaid allotment of shares is not material in nature to the Company.
