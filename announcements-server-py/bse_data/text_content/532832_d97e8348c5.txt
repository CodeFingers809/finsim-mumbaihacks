(ii) Mr. Utpal Seth – 17,93,561 Warrants. 
(iii) Mr. Vikas Khandelwal – 1,00,000 Warrants 
 
In accordance with the terms of issue of Warrants, each Warrant is convertible into 1 (one) 
Equity Share of the Company, upon exercise of option by the Warrant-holder, subject to the 
balance consideration equivalent to 75% of the Issue Price having been paid, within a period of 
18 (eighteen) months from the date of allotment of Warrants, in one or more tranches. 
 
Pursuant to (a) exercise of such right for conversion by Sankhya (for entire 22,41,951 
outstanding Warrants held by it); Mr. Utpal Seth (for entire 17,93,561 outstanding Warrants 
held by him) and Mr. Vikas Khandelwal (for entire 1,00,000 outstanding Warrants held by him), 
aggregating to 41,35,512 Warrants (“Exercised Warrants”); and (b) receipt of balance 
consideration aggregating to INR 34,58,63,207.34/- (Indian Rupees Thirty-Four Crore Fifty-
Eight Lakh Sixty-Three Thousand Two Hundred Seven and Three Four Paisa only), being 75% 
of the Issue Price on these Exercised Warrants from respective warrant-holders, the Company 
has today i.e. on November 20, 2025, issued and allotted 41,35,512 Equity Shares, at an Issue 
Price.
