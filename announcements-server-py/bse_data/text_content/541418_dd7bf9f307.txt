Sub: - Outcome of the Meeting of Board of Directors of the Company held on 14t November, 2025 
Dear Sir/Madam, 
Pursuant to the Regulation 30, 33 and other applicable regulations of SEBI (Listing Obligation and 
Disclosure Requirement) Regulations, 2015, we hereby inform you that the Board of Directors of the 
company in their meeting held today on Friday 14th November, 2025 have inter alia considered and 
approved the following matters: - 
1. Approved the Un-Audited Financial Results of the company for the Second Quarter and Half Year 
Ended on 30th September, 2025 along with Limited Review Report. The statutory auditors have 
expressed an unmodified audit opinion on these results. 
2. With reference to our earlier intimation dated November 06, 2025, we hereby inform you that 
pursuant to the Rights Issue and allotment of partly paid up Rights Equity Shares of the Company 
in the year 2024, the First, Second and Final Call Money was received for 48,12,910 equity shares 
out of 50,90,056 equity shares, which were converted into fully paid up Equity Shares of the 
Company. Thus, on total 2,77,146 partly paid up shares, the Company has not received the call 
money on time. 
Further, the shareholders were intimated through various demand as well as reminder cum 
forfeiture notices requesting them to make the payment of outstanding call money within the 
due date / Final date vide First Call Money Notice dated August 24, 2024, First Reminder for 
First Call Notice dated December 04, 2024, Second Reminder for First Call Notice dated March 
08, 2025 and Second and Final Call Money Notice dated May 15, 2025.The Company intimated 
to the shareholders in the notice and in the reminder notice(s) that non-payment of call money 
would attract forfeiture. 
The receipt of First Call Money on 1,35,402 shares and the receipt of Second and Final Call Money 
on 1,41,744 shares still remained unpaid.
